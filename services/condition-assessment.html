Condition Assessment

Objective visual surveys, non-destructive testing, and detailed reporting that establish the true condition of your structure — the essential first step before any repair decision.

Request an Assessment All Services
Introduction

Understanding the true condition of a structure is the first step to protecting it. Every effective repair, strengthening, or maintenance strategy begins with an accurate, evidence-based picture of how a structure is performing today — and why. CDE's condition assessments provide exactly that: an objective, technically robust evaluation of structural health, free from commercial bias toward any particular repair product or method.

Our engineers combine detailed visual inspection with targeted, non-destructive and semi-destructive testing to identify deterioration, quantify its extent, and trace it back to its root cause. The result is a clear, actionable report that gives owners, consultants, and facility managers the confidence to make informed decisions about their assets.

What a Condition Assessment Covers

Each assessment is scoped to the structure and the concerns at hand, but a typical investigation includes:

  • Visual condition survey — systematic mapping of cracking, spalling, staining, deflection, and other visible distress.
  • Non-destructive testing — cover meter surveys, half-cell potential mapping, rebound hammer, and ultrasonic pulse velocity to assess concrete and reinforcement condition without damage.
  • Material sampling & laboratory testing — core extraction, carbonation depth, chloride and sulphate content, and compressive strength verification.
  • Structural review — comparison of observed condition against original design intent and current loading.
  • Findings & recommendations — a prioritised report defining the cause, severity, and recommended course of action.

Why It Matters

Deterioration rarely announces itself until it is advanced. A structured condition assessment catches problems early, distinguishes cosmetic issues from those that threaten safety or service life, and prevents costly, misdirected repairs. Whether you are planning a transaction, responding to visible distress, or setting a long-term maintenance budget, the assessment gives you a defensible technical basis for every decision that follows.

Our Approach

CDE works as an independent engineering consultancy. We are not tied to any repair contractor or product supplier, so our findings reflect the structure's real needs — nothing more, nothing less. Every assessment is engineer-led from site to report, and our recommendations are always framed around extending service life safely and cost-effectively.
