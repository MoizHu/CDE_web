Repair Design & Specifications

Diagnosis-led repair solutions, material recommendations, and precise specifications that restore structural performance, durability, and serviceability.

Request an Assessment All Services
Introduction

Repairing a structure requires more than addressing visible damage. It demands a clear understanding of the causes of deterioration and the long-term behaviour of materials under environmental exposure. A repair that treats symptoms alone will fail again — often faster than the original defect appeared. CDE approaches repair design as an engineering discipline in its own right: the process of developing solutions that restore structural performance, durability, and serviceability for the full remaining life of the asset.

Every design we produce begins with evidence. By grounding repair strategies in condition assessment and root-cause analysis, we ensure that the work addresses the actual problem rather than its most obvious symptom. The outcome is a set of drawings, specifications, and method statements that give owners, consultants, and contractors a single, unambiguous basis for delivering durable and reliable repairs.

Our Design Methodology

Our repair designs are built on four interconnected principles that carry a project from diagnosis through to a specification a contractor can build to:

  • Diagnosis-based design — every repair strategy is founded on a detailed condition assessment and root-cause analysis, so the design resolves the underlying mechanism of deterioration, not just its visible effects.
  • Material selection — repair materials are chosen for compatibility with the existing construction, the required durability performance, and the specific environmental exposure the structure faces.
  • Methodology development — detailed repair procedures and method statements are prepared to guide proper execution on site and ensure consistency across the works.
  • Detailing & specifications — precise drawings and technical specifications are produced to remove ambiguity and safeguard the quality of implementation.

Scope of Services

Our repair design commissions are scoped to the structure and the deterioration in question, and typically encompass the development of the repair strategy, the preparation of detailed drawings and specifications, material selection and recommendations, and the method statements needed to control execution. Each deliverable is coordinated so that the intent established during diagnosis is preserved all the way to the site.

Applications

CDE prepares repair designs across the full range of deterioration and defect types encountered in reinforced concrete and structural systems, including concrete deterioration, corrosion-related damage, structural defects, and larger rehabilitation projects. Whether the concern is a localised defect or the renewal of an ageing structure, the design is tailored to the mechanisms actually at work.

Outcomes You Can Rely On

A well-conceived repair design delivers more than a return to appearance. Because our solutions target and eliminate root causes, they produce durable, reliable repairs and measurably improved long-term performance. As an independent engineering consultancy, CDE is not tied to any repair product or contractor, so our specifications reflect the structure's real needs and give you a defensible, product-neutral basis for procurement and construction.
