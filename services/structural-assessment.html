Structural Assessment & Strengthening

Evidence-based evaluation of load-carrying capacity and behaviour, paired with targeted strengthening design that helps existing structures safely meet new demands.

Request an Assessment All Services
Introduction

Structural assessment is the process of evaluating the load-carrying capacity, stability, and behaviour of a structure under existing and anticipated conditions. Over time, buildings and infrastructure are called upon to perform beyond their original specifications — whether through ageing, increased loading, changes in use, or environmental exposure. CDE's structural assessment and strengthening service establishes exactly how a structure is performing today and, where necessary, defines the engineering measures needed to close the gap between demand and capacity.

Our work follows a performance-based methodology that accounts for real-world operational context rather than design assumptions alone. Every assessment is engineer-led and independent, so the findings and any strengthening proposals reflect the genuine needs of the structure — extending service life safely, economically, and in compliance with applicable standards.

Our Assessment Methodology

Each engagement is scoped to the structure and the questions being asked of it, but our assessments follow a consistent, staged approach:

  • Data collection — gathering original drawings, material properties, usage data, site observations, and historical records to build an accurate picture of the structure.
  • Structural analysis — using advanced analytical tools to evaluate load paths, stress distribution, and deformation behaviour under existing and anticipated loading.
  • Capacity evaluation — assessing the structure against applicable standards for both current and future load requirements.
  • Deficiency identification — locating weak zones, overstressed elements, and potential points of failure.
  • Strengthening strategy development — defining practical, code-compliant solutions that address each identified deficiency.

Strengthening Design

Where an assessment identifies elements that fall short of required capacity, CDE develops strengthening solutions tailored to the structure, its loading, and its exposure. Depending on the deficiency, these may include carbon-fibre reinforcement, steel plate bonding, section enlargement, or load-redistribution techniques. Each solution is supported by structural modelling, technical drawings, and specifications, giving contractors and consultants a clear, buildable basis for implementation.

Why It Matters

A structure that has changed in use, carried added load, or simply aged may retain hidden reserves — or hidden weaknesses. A rigorous assessment distinguishes the two, preventing both unnecessary intervention and unsafe assumptions. The outcome is enhanced structural safety, improved load capacity, demonstrable standards compliance, and extended asset service life, achieved through cost-effective performance enhancement rather than premature replacement.

Our Approach

CDE works as an independent engineering consultancy. We are not tied to any strengthening product or contractor, so our recommendations are driven solely by what the structure requires. From initial data collection through analysis, capacity evaluation, and strengthening design, every stage is led by our engineers and framed around performance, safety, and long-term value.
