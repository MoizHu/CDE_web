Existing Structures

Assessment, repair, and rehabilitation for structures already in service — restoring performance and extending service life through objective, engineer-led diagnosis.

Structures already in service carry the accumulated effects of environmental exposure, loading, and age. Before any repair is specified, CDE establishes the true condition of the structure — what has deteriorated, how far it has progressed, and why. Accurate diagnosis of the underlying cause is the foundation of every durable intervention.

Our work spans the full assessment-to-execution cycle: condition surveys and non-destructive testing to understand current health, targeted investigation of cracking, corrosion, fire damage, and post-tensioned systems to determine severity and root cause, and structural assessment to evaluate remaining capacity. Findings inform repair strategies and specifications matched to the deterioration mechanism at work.

From there, CDE supervises execution and applies QA/QC to ensure the repair performs as designed, and develops maintenance and lifecycle strategies to protect the investment over the long term. The objective throughout is a technically robust decision — one that restores performance and extends the service life of the structure.
