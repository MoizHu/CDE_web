01 — Our story

A firm built around

CDE Engineering Services is a specialized structural engineering consultancy based in Dubai and operating across the Gulf. We work on existing structures requiring assessment and rehabilitation, and new structures where durability must be designed in from day one.

We are not a general consultancy that includes structural assessment among many services. Durability and structural condition assessment is our entire focus—and that depth of specialization is what clients come back for.
