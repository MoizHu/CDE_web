CDE Engineering Services is a specialized structural engineering consultancy built around one discipline — understanding how structures deteriorate and designing precisely targeted solutions to stop it. We serve clients across the UAE and the wider Middle East from our base in Dubai.

Our Story

A Firm Built Around One Discipline

CDE was established with a clear mandate — to bring genuine specialist expertise to concrete durability and structural assessment in the Middle East. Too often, repair decisions are made without a thorough understanding of the underlying deterioration mechanisms. We exist to change that.

Based in Dubai and operating across the Gulf, we work on both existing structures requiring assessment and rehabilitation, and new structures where durability must be designed in from day one. Every engagement is led by a senior engineer — not delegated down.

What We Stand For

Four Commitments That Define How We Work

These are not aspirational statements. They are the standards we hold ourselves to on every project.

Structural Safety

Every recommendation we make is grounded in verified field data and sound engineering judgment. We never speculate — we investigate.

Engineering Excellence

Our reports are technically rigorous and peer-reviewed. We hold our documentation to publication standard — because clients make decisions based on it.

Environmental Responsibility

Extending the service life of existing structures is the most sustainable engineering decision. We design repairs that last, reducing material consumption and waste.

Long-Term Value Creation

We optimise for lifecycle cost, not just upfront repair cost. Our clients save significantly over 10–20 year horizons by acting on our recommendations.
